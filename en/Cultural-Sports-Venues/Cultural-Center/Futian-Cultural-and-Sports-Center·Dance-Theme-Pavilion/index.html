Park Introduction

Park Introduction

The dance theme hall of Futian Cultural and Sports Center has a construction area of about 4,553 square meters. It has a theme library, dance rehearsal room, multi-functional hall, cultural volunteer station, chorus room, training room, square dance and other regular series of activities. The hall holds regular series of activities such as "Colorful Seasons", "Weekend Dance Flying", "Dancing Forest Colorful Show" art salon all year round. The hall has distinctive dance characteristics and has a number of high-quality teams such as the Flying Dance Youth Dance Troupe and the Autumn Dream Dance Troupe. By establishing the "Wang Yabin Dance Studio", introducing excellent repertoires, creating high-quality dance teams, and creating dance works, it is committed to building a "dance home" that brings together dance artists, enthusiasts and dance workers.

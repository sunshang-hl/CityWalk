## Basic Information

::: tabs#basic-info

@tab:active Park Overview
### Park Overview
- Region: Longhua District
- Park Category: Sports and Fitness Park
- Park Tags: Children's recreational sports
- Detailed Address: Northeast corner of the intersection of Huaming Road and Dalang Park 3rd Road, Longhua District, Shenzhen
- Inquiry Phone: 0755-29672309
- Park Introduction: Dalang Sports Park is located 100 meters southwest of the intersection of Dalang Park 1st Road and Nitouzui, covering an area of 39,000 square meters. It was built in the 1990s. The park has a recreation area for the elderly and children, a basketball court and a fence to facilitate citizens to exercise better. There are seats next to the hiking trail, providing a comfortable resting place for citizens to run and walk.

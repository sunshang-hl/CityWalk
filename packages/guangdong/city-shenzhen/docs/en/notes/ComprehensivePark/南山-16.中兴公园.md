## Basic Information

::: tabs#basic-info

@tab:active Park Overview
### Park Overview
- Region: Nanshan District
- Park Category: City Park
- Park Tags: Humanities and History: Looking Far Away
- Detailed Address: North side of Chuangyan Road, Nanshan District, Shenzhen
- Park Introduction: Zhongxing Park is located at the intersection of Chuangyan Road and Chuangke Road in Xili, Shenzhen, with a total area of about 100,000 square meters, including 58,461 square meters of mountain area and 48,000 square meters of park area, serving about 20,000 people in the surrounding area. The park has a health knowledge promotion area and a health trail.

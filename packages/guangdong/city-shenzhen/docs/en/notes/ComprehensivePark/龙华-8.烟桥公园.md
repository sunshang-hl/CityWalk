### Park Overview
- Region: Longhua District
- Park Category: City Park
- Park Tags: Humanities and History
- Detailed Address: Behind Chen Yanqiao Exhibition Hall, Edi Road, Guangpei Community, Guanlan Street, Longhua District, Shenzhen
- Inquiry Phone: 13682854327

@tab Park Introduction
### Park Introduction
 Yanqiao Park is located in the northeast of the intersection of Edi Road and Yuxin Road in Longhua District. The park has a beautiful environment and is close to Edi Village, the hometown of Mr. Chen Yanqiao, a famous Chinese art educator and printmaker. It has a strong historical atmosphere and presents the new vitality of the old Lingnan style village.

## Basic Information

::: tabs#basic-info

@tab:active Park Overview
### Park Overview
- Region: Bao'an District
- Park Category: City Park
- Park Tags: Children's recreational sports
- Detailed Address: The intersection of Nanchang Road and Gushu 2nd Road, Bao'an District, Shenzhen
- Inquiry Phone: 0755-29662276
- Park Introduction: Nanchang Park is located near Exit F of Gushu Station on Metro Line 1 and belongs to Nanchang Community. The park covers an area of 68,500 square meters.

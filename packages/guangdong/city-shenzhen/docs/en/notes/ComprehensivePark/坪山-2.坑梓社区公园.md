@tab:active Park Overview
### Park Overview
- Region: Pingshan District
- Park Category: City Park
- Park Tags: Sports
- Detailed Address: Next to No. 19, Liyuan Road, Xiuxin Community, Pingshan District
- Inquiry Phone: 18680394628

@tab Park Introduction
### Park Introduction
 Kengzi Park is located in the Shaliyuan residential area of Xiuxin Community. It was built before 2010 and now has a total area of 33,191 square meters. Among them, the park's green area accounts for 11% and the hard ground area accounts for 89%. The park is a secondary management and is positioned as a community park that takes ecological protection as the premise, good green landscape and ecological environment as the theme, and provides citizens with leisure, recreation, sports and fitness. The main design content of the park includes roads, squares, green spaces, structures and supporting facilities.
